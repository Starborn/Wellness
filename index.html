Explore Activities

Forest Therapy (Shinrin-Yoku)

Harness the power of phytoncides and sensory immersion in woodland environments.

Sensory Grounding

Focus on the texture of bark, the scent of damp earth, and the fractal patterns of canopy light.

Guided Stillness

Learn the art of non-striving. Sit in a designated 'spot' for 20 minutes to observe the forest's rhythm.

Ocean Therapy (Blue Health)

Utilizing the rhythmic soundscapes and negative ions of coastal regions.

Blue Mind Meditation

Synchronize your breath with the tidal intervals to lower heart rate and cortisol levels.

Coastal Foraging

A tactile engagement with the shoreline to foster environmental connection and presence.

The Science of Nature

Research indicates that as little as 120 minutes a week in green or blue spaces significantly boosts mental well-being, improves immune function, and reduces blood pressure.
